Christin Swayze

Christin Swayze is the owner of White Mountain Bookkeeping. Christin has been preparing tax returns for more than 15 years. After graduating from Oregon State University in 1997, she sat for and passed the CPA exam. She worked as a CPA from 1997 through 2007 preparing tax returns, doing audits, business valuations, and litigation work. She then went back to college and spent the next ten years teaching business and mathematics in high school and middle school. During this time, Christin chose to no longer keep her CPA license. In 2018, she became an Enrolled Agent and Licensed Tax Consultant in Oregon. She worked as an Office Manager for H&R Block in 2019 and 2020. For the tax seasons 2021 through 2023, she worked for small tax practitioners in Oregon and then in Arizona. Christin is a current member of the National Association of Tax Professionals (NATP) and the Oregon Association of Tax Consultants (OATC).
