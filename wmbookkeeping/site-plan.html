Purpose

The purpose of the website is to provide information about a company that provides bookkeeping and tax services. This company will be located in Concho, Arizona and provide services for the White Mountain area predominantely. This website will show what services are available and how the owner can be contacted.

Audience

The audience that this website is intended for are business owners in the White Mountain area that are in need of Bookkeeping and tax services. The intended audience is taxpayers with businesses, but could be any taxpayer as I can also do indiviual tax returns. They could also be of any income bracket.

Home page

Goal White Mountain Bookkeeping, LLC (WMB) is a full-service bookkeeping and tax company in the White Mountain area of Arizona. WMB specializes in bookkeeping and financial statements for small businesses, tax return filing and tax planning for individuals, and corporate tax return filing. Bookkeeping services are tailored to each business's unique needs. Our goal is to help you with your bookkeeping activities so you can concentrate on growing your business. We will do this by providing you with timely financial information, how you want it. Why Outsource? Outsourcing your bookkeeping needs can save you money, help you have less stress, and give you please of mind. You can save money because outsourcing generally costs a fraction of the money that hiring an employee does. Then when you go home at night you can relax knowing that your bookkeeping tasks are being done for you by someone else. You can have peace of mind knowing that someone else is watching over your bookkeeping tasks that has experience and knowledge of how to prepare your books and/or tax returns for outside experts. Services

Bookkeeping and Accounting Services The services White Mountain Bookkeeping (WMB) provides include bookkeeping and tax preparation. For bookkeeping and accounting purposes there will be weekly, monthly, and quarterly services available. I will also be able to work directly with your tax preparer if you would prefer to keep bookkeeping and tax services separate. Services include payroll, sales tax, adding and categorizing financial transactions, financial statements, bank/loan/credit card reconciliation, workers compensation audits, accounts payable and accounts receivables, and tax projections. For start-ups I can assist with new Quickbooks set up, provide Quickbooks training, and give guidance on record keeping for tax preparation purposes. If you have fallen behind in your accounting, we can help with that too. We can complete basic bookkeeping for previous months or even years to help you get back on track. Then financial statements can be issued to help get those tax returns filed. Whatever services you are in need of, we can help you through support and genuine interest in helping you to grow your business. Tax Preparation Services For tax preparation, individual tax preparation is the core specialty. We have over 15 years of experience preparing and filing returns electronically, including clients that have investments, rentals, and small businesses. No matter what kind of small business you have, including C Corporations, S Corporations, Limited Liability Corporations, Partnerships, and Sole Proprietor we can help prepare the tax return to help minimize the tax owed. Federal and State tax returns have been prepared for restaurants, contractors, truck drivers, daycares, online retailers, and much more. In Person or Virtually If you are in the White Mountain area of Arizona, services could be conducted at your place of business. However, much of the bookkeeping and tax preparation could be done virtually and sent to you through email or other safe portals. Call today for your free consultation.

About

Christin Swayze is the owner of White Mountain Bookkeeping. Christin has been preparing tax returns for more than 30 years. After graduating from Oregon State University in 1997, she sat for and passed the CPA exam. She worked as a CPA from 1997 through 2007 preparing tax returns, doing audits, business valuations, and litigation work. She then went back to college and spent the next ten years teaching business and mathematics in high school and middle school. During this time, Christin chose to no longer keep her CPA license. In 2018, she became an Enrolled Agent and Licensed Tax Consultant in Oregon. She worked as an Office Manager for H&R Block in 2019 and 2020. For the tax seasons 2021 through 2023, she worked for small tax practitioners in Oregon and then in Arizona. Christin is a current member of the National Association of Tax Professionals (NATP) and the Oregon Association of Tax Consultants (OATC).
